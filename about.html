About Mpoma School satellite Campus

Mpoma School, Satellite campus (Mixed) is a sister school to Mpomaschool Girl's campus.

Mpoma School, Satellite Campus is located along Kayunga-Bugeree road from Mukono. Just before Mpoma Satellite earth station.
